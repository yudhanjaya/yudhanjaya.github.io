COVER QUOTE

Project 2054 is a scifi anthology brought to you by Yudhanjaya Wijeratne, JT Lawrence, Jason Werbeloff and Colby Rice.
We write in a shared universe inspired by the question:
What would the world look like in the future?
This very first 2054 takes you from South Africa to the USA to Israel to the depths of the Bay of Bengal.

Contains: Deep Ocean Blues, Melting Shlemiel, The Memory Hacker, The Camille.

JT Lawrence
Location: Johannesburg, South Africa

JT Lawrence is an Amazon bestselling speculative fiction author (and playwright) known for her When Tomorrow Calls series. She is the mother of two small boys and a baby girl, and lives in a house with a red front door.

Colby R. Rice
Location: Tucson, United States of America

A shameless nerd and bookworm since the age of five, Colby R. Rice is the author of the Books of Ezekiel. In addition to being a novelist, she's also a film director, game writer and narrative designer.

Jason Werbeloff
Location: Johannesburg, South Africa

Jason Werbeloff is the novelist and philosopher behind the Deframenting Daniel series. His passion is exploring philosophical debate - especially around the nature of social groups - through the lens of science fiction.

Yudhanjaya Wijeratne
Location: Colombo, Sri Lanka

Yudhanjaya Wijeratne is a researcher and jaded ex-journalist who now looks at the world through the lens of Big Data. He wrote Numbercaste and works at the intersection of technology and politics.
